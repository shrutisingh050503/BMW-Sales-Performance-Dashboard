# BMW-Sales-Performance-Dashboard

Problem Statement

The automotive industry is rapidly evolving with changes in consumer demand, electric vehicle adoption, and regional market dynamics. BMW, being a global brand, needs a centralized and interactive system to monitor:

 * Sales performance across regions
 * Revenue trends over time
 * Model-wise contribution
 * Impact of EV (BEV) adoption
 * Growth patterns (MoM & YoY)

Traditional reports make it difficult to quickly identify key trends and decision-making insights.

Dashboard Goal

The goal of this dashboard is to:

* Provide a clear and interactive overview of BMW’s global sales performance
* Track key KPIs like revenue, units sold, and growth
* Analyze regional and model-wise performance
* Monitor electric vehicle (BEV) trends
* Enable data-driven decision making through visual insights

Key Visuals (Brief Explanation)

KPI Cards

* Total Units Sold (25M) → Overall sales volume
* Revenue (2T) → Total earnings
* MoM Growth (0.01) → Monthly performance trend
* YoY Growth (0.16) → Yearly growth indicator
* Avg BEV Share (0.11) → EV adoption level
* Avg Price (63.85K) → Pricing insights

 Provides a quick snapshot of business performance

1. Donut Chart – Market Share View
 * Displays region-wise sales distribution
 * Helps identify dominant markets

2. Treemap – Top Performing Models
 * Shows model-wise performance contribution
 * Highlights best-selling BMW models

3. Line Chart – EV Trend
 * Tracks growth in electric vehicle adoption
 * Shows gradual increase over years

4. Stacked Bar – Region & Model Analysis
 * Compares model performance across regions
 * Helps identify regional preferences

5. Area Chart – Revenue Trend
 * Displays yearly revenue growth
 * Indicates business expansion over time

6. Bar Chart – Units Sold by Model
 * Highlights top and low-performing models
 * Useful for product strategy decisions

7. Interactive Filters (Slicers)
* Year
* Region
* Model

Enables dynamic analysis and user-driven exploration

Impact & Insights

Key Insights

* Consistent Revenue Growth → Strong upward trend indicates business expansion
* EV Adoption Rising → BEV share shows increasing demand for electric vehicles
* Models Driving Sales → Few models contribute significantly to total sales
* Regional Dominance → Some regions outperform others in volume and revenue
* Stable Pricing Strategy → Average price remains consistent

Business Impact

* Helps stakeholders identify high-performing markets and models
* Supports strategic decisions on EV investments
* Enables data-driven marketing and pricing strategies
* Improves performance tracking with real-time insights

Conclusion

This dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights using interactive visuals. It provides a comprehensive view of BMW’s global performance, making it easier to track trends, identify opportunities, and support strategic decision-making.

Tools Used

 * Power BI
 * DAX (for KPIs & calculations)
 * Data Cleaning (Power Query)
